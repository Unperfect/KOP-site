Meet the Photographer...


Kami Owens is a creative natural light photographer who serves in Davis and Weber county Utah.


Hello, I am Kami Owens, the photographer behind KamiO Photography, Photography has always been a hobby/love of mine since I was a child. Although I always loved taking photos, photography truly became my passion when my children were born. Watching them grow up through the lens of my camera made me realize how precious life really is and you need to capture the memories that will last forever.


As an on location photographer, my approach to photography is relaxed, fun, friendly and modern. I love what I do and I am sure you will love what I will capture for you in memories that will last a lifetime. I enjoy taking creative and beautiful photographs..


I enjoy making my sessions fun, enjoyable, having conversations with my clients so I just seem like some person not interested at all with clients. You make my job so that I need you just as much as you need me! I hope to have the opportunity to meet you!!
